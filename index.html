Our Story

Crafted with Passion,
Served with Love

What started as a passion for healthy, natural beverages has grown into a beloved local brand. Our journey began in a small kitchen, experimenting with hibiscus flowers and traditional recipes passed down through generations.

Today, we're proud to serve our signature zobo drink to hundreds of happy customers. But we're just getting started. Our dream is to bring you a full range of refreshing fruit-based drinks that celebrate nature's sweetness.
